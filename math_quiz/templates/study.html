Math Study Page

Addition

Addition is the process of combining two or more numbers into a single sum. The symbol for addition is '+'. Here are some tips:

  • Start adding from the rightmost digits if they are lined up by their place values.
  • Remember to carry over if the sum exceeds the value of one digit.

Example: 123 + 456 = 579

Subtraction

Subtraction involves taking one number away from another. The symbol for subtraction is '-'. Some tips:

  • Line up the numbers by their place values.
  • Borrow from the next higher place value if necessary.

Example: 645 - 123 = 522

Multiplication

Multiplication is a shortcut for repeated addition. The symbol for multiplication is '×'.

  • Multiply numbers starting from the rightmost digit.
  • Sum up all the partial products to get the final result.

Example: 123 × 4 = 492
